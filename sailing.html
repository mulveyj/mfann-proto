Sailing and the Sea

A little over ten years ago, with only the slightest of sailing experience, I made the somewhat foolhardy decision to become a crewmember on the 2007/08 Clipper Round the World Yacht Race. I didn't circumnavigate completely, but spent six months on Singapore Clipper (under the steady command of skipper Mark Preedy), travelling all the way to Australia and (later) from San Francisco back to the UK. A challenging (sometimes gruelling) experience, it nevertheless cemented my long-term love of the sea and all things nautical.

These days, my seafaring adventures are a little more staid, the highlight usually being a leisurely flotilla holiday in the Med. However, as my children become properly indoctrinated in all matters marine, perhaps the prospect of another ocean trip will become more likely...
