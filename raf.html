RAF Experience

I joined the RAF in 1993 and passed the Airman Aircrew Initial Training Course to become one of the first female non-commissioned aircrew to serve on an operational squadron. I spent ten years as a loadmaster with the rank of Sergeant, serving in 33 Sqn and 230 Sqn, both of which were equipped with the Puma HC1. I saw active duty in Bosnia (Operation Grapple) with 33 Squadron and in Northern Ireland (230 Squadron). I participated in numerous exercises in a range of environments including Arctic warfare in Norway and jungle environments.
